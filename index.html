VIBE: Video Instruction-aligned Background music gEneration.

Qualitative comparison of generated music across models for the same video input, with additional studies on instruction following and rhythmic alignment. Best experienced with headphones.

Instruction-following — same video, different prompts yield distinct musical outputs.
Rhythm-aware — generated music aligns to motion and beat cues from the video.
Dual-mode — supports both video-to-music and text-to-music generation.

03 · Instruction following

Same video, different prompts.

Fixing the video and varying only the text prompt produces meaningfully different musical outputs from VIBE — demonstrating that the model attends to the textual instruction rather than collapsing to a single video-conditioned mode.
